about us

amul the taste of india

The journey of Amul Ice Cream began on the 10th March, 1996 in Gujarat and with its superior quality it was able to capture the highest market share in a short period of time. In 1997, Amul Ice Creams entered Mumbai followed by Chennai in 1998 and Kolkata and Delhi in 2002. Nationally it was rolled out in 1999.Amul has always brought newness in its products and the same applies for its ice creams. Not only has it grown at a phenomenal rate but also it has added a vast variety of flavours to its ever growing range. Currently it offers a selection of 220 products. In January 2007, Amul introduced Sugar Free & ProLife Probiotic Wellness Ice Cream, which was first-of-its-kind in India for the health conscious.

Ingredients Amul ice cream is made with real milk and cream, and contains at least 10% milk fat. It does not contain any added vegetable oil. Quality Amul ice cream is made in ISO and HACCP certified plants that follow strict hygienic standards. Amul ice cream is made with higher standards than those specified by PFA and BIS, resulting in less air and more fat for a creamier texture. Flavors Amul ice cream comes in a variety of flavors, including frozen yogurt, lactose free, sugar free, sticks, and Sweetheart. Health Amul was the first company in India to introduce probiotics and sugar free ice cream. History Amul ice cream was first introduced in Gujarat in 1996 and quickly gained market share. It was rolled out nationally in 1999. Products Amul ice cream products include sundaes, regular cups, tubes, and party packs. Amul also offers Amul Creme Rich, which is said to be creamier and richer than regular ice cream.
